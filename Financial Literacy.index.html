"The Value-Add Menu"

To ensure this program has a measurable impact for your ESG reporting, we recommend including these high-value components.

Confidential Financial Health Clinics (Highly Recommended)

What it is:

After each monthly session, I will make myself available for 4-6 bookable, 30-minute 1-on-1 "clinic" slots.

Why it matters:

This is a safe, confidential space for employees to ask personal questions they would never ask in a group. (e.g., "Here is my debt statement, can you help me?").

ESG Impact:

This is the single most effective way to reduce employee financial stress.

Targeted "Life-Stage" Workshops

What it is:

Two (2) specialized 90-minute workshops during the year, aimed at specific demographics.

Suggested Topics:

  • For New Hires (Gen Z): "My First Paycheck: 5 Smart Money Moves to Make in Your 20s."
  • For Pre-Retirees (Age 50+): "From Work to What's Next: Navigating Your EPF Withdrawal & Post-Retirement Income."

The Digital Resource Hub

What it is:

A simple monthly e-newsletter that includes:

  • A summary of the month's topic.
  • A simple tool (e.g., budget template, debt calculator).
  • A "Financial Term of the Month."

Why it matters:

Provides continuous, passive engagement and reinforces learning for all employees, even those who missed a session.
